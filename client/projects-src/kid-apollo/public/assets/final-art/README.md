# Kid Apollo final art pack

This folder is the landing zone for approved or commissioned final assets.

Do not treat downloaded marketplace or CC0 material as final Kid Apollo art.

## Served base path

/assets/final-art/

## Folder contract

- rocket/ = custom final rocket model, source files, textures, decals
- goat/ = custom goat model, rig, animation clips, textures
- room/ = custom music-room blockout and final room model
- decals/ = KA marks, labels, stickers, repair patches
- poster/ = poster-mode source and exports
- sfx/ = final sound design
- exports/ = production-ready optimised outputs
- _lookdev-inputs/ = temporary material copied from the moodboard pack

## Boundary

`_lookdev-inputs` are references and temporary look-dev inputs only.

Final identity remains custom:
rocket, goat, porthole, room composition, decals, poster still, and final SFX.
